Packguin

We are a friendly, supportive and educational mobile-app for users who’re moving into a new home. Intent on providing direct, organized information to reinforce user confidence and decision making.

My Role

UI/UX Designer

UX Researcher

Team

University Group Project

Timeline

14 weeks

Tools

Figma

Photoshop

Problem

First-time movers often feel overwhelmed by scattered and hard-to-navigate information. This leads to confusion, stress, and missed tasks during the moving process.

Solution

By centralizing valuable moving information we can re-organize it in a user-friendly manner. Focused on displaying it from our mascot, Poppy, we can provide the user with a stress free environment to enhance decision making and build confidence.

Research Snapshot

63%

of first time-movers did not make a budget

70%

of first time-movers felt they had no guide for moving

81%

of first-time movers rely on friends & family for advice

43%

of first-time movers had fears about their finances

Launch

This landing page is designed to reduce overwhelm by immediately orienting users with their current progress and next steps. A personalized greeting, progress indicator, and a clear call-to-action guide users seamlessly back into their moving plan.
